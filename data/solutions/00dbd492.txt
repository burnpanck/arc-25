- Input consists of one or more red squares with a red dot in the centre.
- Output is the same, with the black space between the rectangle and its centre filled with a different color.
- The color is determined by the size of the rectangle. Specifically:
  * 5x5 rectangles are filled in cyan
  * 7x7 rectangles are filled in yellow
  * 9x9 rectangles are filled in green
